Chat for interaction with the user of your website.

== Description ==

With Online Chat, users can speak directly to you as they browse the site,
this facilitates the conversation between the parties, improving the average time needed to complete your purchase or question!

To work you must have a registration on https://criasystem.tech and configure the chat token.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to configure chat? =

1 - Log on to the https://criasystem.tech platform;
2 - Click on chat on the left menu;
3 - If the chat is not created, click on new, otherwise skip to step 5;
4 - Fill in the information and save;
5 - Click on the WordPress icon to copy the token;
For more information, contact us through the website.
